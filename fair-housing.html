Federal Fair Housing Act

We do not discriminate on the basis of race, color, religion, sex, disability, familial status, or national origin. We strive to ensure that our advertising, client interactions, showing practices, and referral activities comply with the Federal Fair Housing Act and related guidance.

Property-Focused Marketing Only

Property descriptions on this website are intended to describe the features of the real estate itself and not the people who may or may not live in a community. Marketing language should remain property-focused and inclusive at all times.

Colorado Fair Housing Protections

In addition to federal law, Colorado law may provide additional protections. The website owner and brokerage expect all business practices to comply with applicable Colorado civil rights, fair housing, and real estate advertising requirements.
