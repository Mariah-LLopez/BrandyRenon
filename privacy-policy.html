Privacy Policy

This policy describes how inquiry information from this real estate website may be collected, used, shared, and protected.

Information We Collect

When you submit a form on this website, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, message contents, and the property or service that prompted your inquiry. We may also collect limited technical information such as browser type, device type, referring pages, and interaction data from analytics tools.

Why Information Is Collected

Information is collected so the agent or brokerage can respond to questions, schedule showings, share listing updates, provide requested market information, and maintain basic lead follow-up records. Information may also be used to improve the website experience, monitor traffic, and support advertising or remarketing campaigns where permitted by law and brokerage policy.

Who Receives Your Information

Inquiry information may be received by the listing agent, the brokerage, authorized staff, transaction coordinators, and approved technology vendors such as CRM providers, business email platforms, website hosting services, analytics providers, and secure form-processing tools. Data is shared only as reasonably necessary to respond to the inquiry or operate the business.

Analytics, Cookies, and Tracking Technologies

This website may use cookies, analytics tags, pixels, session logs, and similar tracking technologies to understand usage patterns, measure campaign performance, and improve marketing effectiveness. Browser settings may allow you to limit some tracking, but certain site features may not work as intended if tracking technologies are disabled.

Form Data Handling

Form submissions are intended for private business communication and are not publicly displayed on the website. Submitted information should be routed through a secure CRM, email service, or hosting provider with appropriate access controls. No submission data should ever be stored in publicly accessible files.

Important Submission Warning

Please do not submit Social Security numbers, driver's license numbers, bank account details, credit card information, or other highly sensitive financial or identity information through website forms. This website is intended for general real estate inquiries only.
